Our Funds

For investors who prefer a fund structure — whether for diversification, tax efficiency, or operational convenience — Wealtheon offers four regulated investment funds.

Global Equity · Growth

Protea Fund Growth World Equity

A broadly diversified global equity fund targeting high-growth companies across developed and emerging markets. The fund applies Wealtheon's Growth investment philosophy at scale, providing investors with efficient international equity exposure within a regulated UCITS structure.

Value · DBI-RDT Tax Regime

DBI-RDT Value Fund

A value-oriented equity fund structured to qualify under the Belgian DBI-RDT (definitief belaste inkomsten / revenus définitivement taxés) regime. This makes it particularly attractive for Belgian corporate investors seeking to hold dividend-paying equities in a tax-efficient wrapper.

Multi-Asset · Best Ideas

High Conviction Fund

The fund equivalent of our High Conviction direct line — a concentrated portfolio of 15 to 25 positions representing our very best cross-asset ideas. Available to investors who want concentrated, differentiated performance in a regulated and accessible vehicle.

Defensive Equity · Income

Value Fund

A diversified equity fund investing in quality companies trading below intrinsic value. The fund prioritises capital preservation and consistent returns over the long term, with an emphasis on cash-generative businesses and attractive dividend income.
