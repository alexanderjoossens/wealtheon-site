About us

Wealtheon is the fruitful result of two generations of experience in asset management. We manage your assets with the greatest care — today, for tomorrow.

Two generations of experience, working for you

Wealtheon is the fruitful result of two generations of experience in asset management. We manage your assets with the greatest care, today, for tomorrow — guided by a long-term vision rather than the noise of the next quarter.

Experience shows that the better our team informs you, the more comfortable you feel. So you can rely on that as well. We work through open and transparent communication, because, after all, your interests are also ours.

We, as families, invest alongside our clients — Victor in the growth strategy and Naard in the value strategy. As shareholders, we are closely involved with every individual client and with the assets they entrust to Wealtheon. From Brussels, Eindhoven and Waregem, Victor and Naard, together with the Wealtheon team, are ready to take care of your asset management. Share your challenges with us and we will assist you.

"We manage your portfolio as a good steward — today, for the day of tomorrow. Through open and transparent communication, because your interests are also ours."
Victor Zwart & Naard Broeckaert

Your value, our experience, for common growth. As an independent asset manager with a long-term vision, our mission is simple: to grow and protect your wealth, and to guide you from A to Z — at all times, even if your situation changes along the way.

Growth and protection

Wealtheon is your independent asset manager with a long-term vision, aiming at attractive results and appropriate risk. As a seasoned partner, always close to you as our customer, we guide you from A to Z — even if your situation changes in the meantime. We are here to advise you.

01

Growth

We invest for attractive, long-term results. With a clear vision and the discipline of two generations of experience, our goal is to grow your wealth steadily — today, for tomorrow.

02

Protection

Growth only matters when the risk is right. We manage your assets with the greatest care, balancing every opportunity against appropriate, well-considered risk so your capital is protected.

03

Independence

As an independent asset manager, we have no product sales targets and no hidden agendas. Our only objective is yours — and that independence is the foundation of everything we do.

04

Partnership

As a seasoned partner, we stay close to you. We guide you from A to Z and remain at your side even when your situation changes — through open, transparent communication, because we share the same interests.

Family-owned. Independent. Committed.

Being a family-owned firm means our time horizon is measured in generations, not fiscal quarters. We have no external shareholders demanding short-term returns. We answer to our clients — and to the families who built this firm. That independence is the foundation of everything we do.

Governance & Compliance

We operate under strict regulatory oversight in Belgium and the Netherlands. Compliance is not a box-ticking exercise at Wealtheon — it is an expression of our commitment to integrity and the protection of our clients.

Belgium & Netherlands

EU Regulatory Framework

Wealtheon is regulated under Belgian financial services legislation and operates within the EU MiFID II framework. We are registered in Belgium with VAT number BE0423.072.032 (UID 17223344). Client assets are held in segregated accounts at regulated custodian banks.

  • MiFID II compliant investment services
  • GDPR data protection standards
  • Annual independent audit of client reporting
  • Segregated client asset custody
Transparency

Open & aligned by design

As an independent, family-owned asset manager we have no product sales targets and no conflicts of interest. We communicate openly and transparently, because we share the same interests as our clients — your value, our experience, for common growth.

  • Independent — no bank affiliation
  • Clear, transparent reporting
  • Long-term alignment with clients
  • Guidance from A to Z, at all times
