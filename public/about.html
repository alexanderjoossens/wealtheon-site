About Wealtheon

Two generations of expertise. A family-owned business with a long-term vision. We manage assets with the greatest care — today, for tomorrow.

Our story

An independent firm built for the long term

Wealtheon was founded on a simple but powerful belief: that clients deserve an asset manager who thinks in decades, not quarters. As an independent firm, we have no bank affiliation, no product sales targets, and no conflicts of interest. Our only objective is to grow and protect our clients' wealth.

Our family heritage means that the founders and their successors remain actively involved in managing assets day to day. This continuity — spanning more than two generations — creates a depth of institutional knowledge and a culture of accountability that is genuinely rare in asset management.

We are active across Belgium, the Netherlands, and Switzerland, with a team of dedicated investment professionals, analysts, and client advisers working together from four offices.

"With more than two generations of experience in asset management, we dispose of a lot of knowledge to advise you. We are a family business — and we invest like it."
The Wealtheon Family

Our mission is to manage the assets of our clients with the greatest care today, for tomorrow. We are an independent asset manager with a long-term vision — a family-owned business where two generations of expertise serve your financial future.

Our principles

What We Stand For

These four values are not aspirational statements — they are the standards by which we hold ourselves accountable, every day.

01

Client First

At every level of our organisation, we strive to constantly exceed your expectations. Your interests are the first — and only — consideration in every decision we make. We measure our success by yours.

02

Integrity

As a family-owned business active in the financial sector, we attach great importance to our reputation. We act at all times in a trustworthy and transparent way towards all stakeholders — clients, counterparties, regulators, and staff.

03

Aligned Interests

Together with you, we co-invest in our investment strategies for the long term. Wealtheon staff invest their own capital alongside our clients' — because we believe fully in the approaches we manage.

04

Expertise

As a knowledge-driven organisation, we continuously build on our expertise spanning decades. Our investment team brings together deep sector knowledge, quantitative rigour, and the qualitative judgement that only experience can develop.

Family-owned. Independent. Committed.

Being a family-owned firm means our time horizon is measured in generations, not fiscal quarters. We have no external shareholders demanding short-term returns. We answer to our clients — and to the families who built this firm. That independence is the foundation of everything we do.

Regulatory framework

Governance & Compliance

We operate under strict regulatory oversight in each of our jurisdictions. Compliance is not a box-ticking exercise at Wealtheon — it is an expression of our commitment to integrity and the protection of our clients.

Belgium & Netherlands

EU Regulatory Framework

Wealtheon SA is regulated under Belgian financial services legislation and operates within the EU MiFID II framework. We are registered in Belgium with VAT number BE0423.072.032. Client assets are held in segregated accounts at regulated custodian banks.

  • MiFID II compliant investment services
  • GDPR data protection standards
  • Annual independent audit of client reporting
  • Segregated client asset custody
Switzerland

Swiss Regulatory Framework

Wealtheon SA (Geneva) operates under Swiss financial law with authorisation from FINMA. We are supervised by AOOS (Swiss Limited Corporation for Supervision), a member of SAAM (Swiss Asset Managers Association), and subject to OFS (Ombud Finance Switzerland).

  • Authorisation: FINMA Swiss Financial Market Supervisory Authority
  • Supervision: AOOS Swiss Limited Corporation for Supervision
  • Membership: SAAM Swiss Asset Managers Association
  • Ombudsman: OFS Ombud Finance Switzerland
