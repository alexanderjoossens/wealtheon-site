Responsible investing

Our Commitment to Impact

At Wealtheon, responsible investment is not a marketing label — it is an integral part of our investment process. We incorporate environmental, social, and governance (ESG) factors into our analysis because we believe that sustainable businesses generate superior long-term returns.

Through the Wealtheon Foundation, we also support community and philanthropic initiatives that reflect our values as a family-owned firm with a genuine long-term perspective.

Our impact & ESG approach  →

Wealtheon Foundation

We believe that financial success and social responsibility go hand in hand. Through our foundation, we direct a portion of our revenues towards initiatives that create lasting positive impact — for communities, for the environment, and for future generations.

ESG Integration

Our portfolio managers apply ESG screening and engagement across all strategies. We seek companies that manage their environmental footprint, govern themselves with integrity, and treat their stakeholders fairly.
