Our Investment Strategies

We work with three core strategies, each tailored to different risk profiles and investment horizons. Every mandate is built around your objectives.

Growth

Offensive

Growth stocks expected to outpace the market average. Typical for young, promising companies with strong turnover growth and substantial reinvestment. We seek tomorrow's leaders today.

Value

Defensive

Our story

A family business built on trust

Wealtheon is an independent asset manager with a long-term vision. We manage assets with the greatest care today, for tomorrow. Our firm was founded on the conviction that clients deserve a manager who thinks in generations, not quarters.

With more than two generations of experience in asset management, we bring a depth of knowledge that spans market cycles, economic shifts, and structural changes across Europe. This continuity of expertise is rare — and it is at the core of what makes Wealtheon different.
