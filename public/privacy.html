Privacy Policy

This policy explains what personal data Wealtheon collects, why we process it, the legal basis for doing so, and the rights you have. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently and securely.

Last updated: 28 June 2026

Wealtheon SA/NV ("Wealtheon", "we", "us") is a Belgian investment firm, authorised and supervised by the FSMA, with offices in Belgium, the Netherlands and Switzerland. We are the controller responsible for the personal data described in this policy.

2. What personal data we collect

Depending on how you interact with us, we may process:

  • Contact and enquiry data — your name, email address, phone number and the content of any message you send us through our contact form or by email.
  • Client and prospect data — information required to provide our services and meet legal obligations (identification, financial and suitability information), where you become a client.
  • Recruitment data — any details you share when applying for a position via our Careers page.
  • Technical and usage data — IP address, browser type, device information and pages visited, collected automatically when you use our website.
  • Cookie data — see section 6.

We do not knowingly collect data from children, and we do not collect special categories of personal data through this website.

3. Why we use your data and the legal basis

We process personal data under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable Belgian, Dutch and Swiss law, on the following bases:

  • To respond to your enquiries — based on your consent and our legitimate interest in answering you.
  • To provide our services and manage our client relationship — based on the performance of a contract.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations — including financial-services, anti-money-laundering and MiFID II requirements — based on our legal obligations.
  • To operate, secure and improve our website — based on our legitimate interest.
  • To handle job applications — based on steps taken at your request prior to a possible contract.

4. LinkedIn content on our website

Our "News" page displays posts published by our own official LinkedIn company page (Wealtheon Asset Management). To do this we use LinkedIn's official Community Management API in accordance with the LinkedIn Marketing API Terms — we do not scrape LinkedIn.

We retrieve and display only content that we ourselves have published on our company page: the text of the post, any images we attached, the publication date and a link back to the original post on LinkedIn. We do not collect, store or display personal data of individual members who like, comment on or otherwise engage with those posts.

Access is authorised by an administrator of our LinkedIn page through LinkedIn's OAuth flow, and the resulting access credentials are stored securely and used solely to fetch our own published posts. If you interact with us on LinkedIn directly, LinkedIn's own Privacy Policy applies to that processing.

5. How we share your data

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with:

  • Service providers who process data on our behalf under contract — for example our website hosting and IT providers — strictly to deliver their services to us.
  • Regulators, authorities and professional advisers where required by law or to protect our legal rights.

All processors are bound by appropriate confidentiality and data-protection obligations.

6. Cookies and analytics

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies that are necessary for the site to function and, where you consent, to understand how the site is used so we can improve it. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect how the website works.

7. International transfers

Wealtheon operates in Belgium, the Netherlands and Switzerland, and some of our service providers may process data outside the European Economic Area. Where data is transferred internationally, we put in place appropriate safeguards (such as the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses or an adequacy decision) to protect it.

8. How long we keep your data

We keep personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes set out above, and to meet legal, regulatory, accounting or reporting requirements. Retention periods for client data are governed by applicable financial-services legislation. Enquiry and recruitment data are kept only as long as needed to handle your request, unless you agree otherwise.

9. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you have the right to: access your personal data; have it corrected or deleted; restrict or object to its processing; receive your data in a portable format; and, where processing is based on consent, withdraw that consent at any time without affecting prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, email info@wealtheon.eu. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority:

  • Belgium — Gegevensbeschermingsautoriteit / Autorité de protection des données, www.gegevensbeschermingsautoriteit.be
  • Netherlands — Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens, www.autoriteitpersoonsgegevens.nl
  • Switzerland — Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner (FDPIC), www.edoeb.admin.ch

10. Security

We apply appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, loss, misuse or alteration. Access to data and to any third-party API credentials is restricted to authorised personnel and systems.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version is always available on this page, with the "Last updated" date shown above.

12. Contact
