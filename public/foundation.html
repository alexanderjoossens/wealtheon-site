Investing together, effectively, for a better world

The Wealtheon Foundation believes in the butterfly effect: small, well-considered changes can, in time, have a major impact on society. On behalf of our donors — and in our own capacity — we invest directly in special projects and good causes that genuinely make a difference.

Our philosophy

Together, we invest directly in building a better world

For many years, Wealtheon Asset Management has donated to good causes and projects in a specific manner and following a specific philosophy. Besides providing financial support, we also work to raise general awareness — because we believe that investing together in a better world brings rewards for all parties involved.

To increase our impact and bring structure to the way projects are selected and monitored, our contributions are combined with those of our donors in the Wealtheon Foundation. This allows other philanthropists to benefit directly from Wealtheon's experience and expertise.

Rather than focusing on a single cause, the Foundation offers structural, lasting financial support to a carefully chosen set of charities, across six focus areas — at both a national and an international level.

"Every well-considered deed matters. Small changes can, in time, have a major impact on society — and investing together in a better world brings rewards for all."
The Wealtheon Foundation

What we stand for

Our Ambitions

The Wealtheon Foundation seeks to help its donors achieve their philanthropic ambitions — by investing together, directly, in carefully selected partner charities, and by making sure every donation is spent effectively.

01

Greater impact, together

By combining the contributions of Wealtheon and its donors, and by developing lasting relationships with the charities we support, we achieve more than any of us could alone.

02

A structured donation policy

We offer a structured way to give and regularly evaluate the good causes we have selected, ensuring donations are always spent where they are needed most.

03

Close monitoring

To be sure investments end up in the right place, we closely follow how each cause uses its donations and the progress it makes towards its goals.

04

Lasting partnerships

We regularly bring together our own clients and the philanthropic organisations we support, so that — over time — we can truly make a difference together.

Where we invest

The Six Themes of the Foundation

Rather than focusing on a single project, we offer structural support to reputable institutions with a long history as well as promising new initiatives. Our selection starts from these six themes.

01 — Children & education

A foundation for social progress

Good education lets everyone make the most of their talents. We support the Barodia school project in India, providing food, tuition and technology skills to over 150 underprivileged children every day — with priority for girls and the most vulnerable. Wealtheon has been involved since the project's launch in 2007.

  • Barodia school project — India
  • Priority for girls and the most vulnerable
  • Daily food, tuition & technology skills
02 — Research

Innovation for the challenges of our time

Investing in a better future means investing in research across food, energy and healthcare. We donate to Diabetes Onderzoek Nederland (DON), focused on a cure for type 1 diabetes, and to the renowned Jules Bordet Institute, a Belgian cancer research centre that depends heavily on private funding.

  • Diabetes Onderzoek Nederland (DON)
  • Jules Bordet Institute — cancer research
  • Fundamental research & innovation
03 — Sustainable development

Progress that does not cost the future

We have a major responsibility to the generations that follow us. We support ODDY-C, founded in 2011 in northern Burundi, which invests in clean water pipes, lifestyle improvements and partnerships with schools in the south of the country.

  • ODDY-C — northern Burundi
  • Clean water infrastructure
  • School partnerships & lifestyle improvement
04 — Art & culture

Essential for innovation and growth

We support Musica Mundi, whose annual course and festival lets talented young musicians aged 10–18 perform alongside eminent interpreters of chamber music. We also back the Queen Elisabeth Competition and the Royal Concertgebouw Orchestra — making classical music accessible to all.

  • Musica Mundi — young international talent
  • Queen Elisabeth Competition
  • Royal Concertgebouw Orchestra
05 — Humanitarian aid

Standing with the victims

Armed conflicts and natural disasters demand our response. We donate to Human Rights Watch, a leading international institute since 1978, which conducts rigorous investigations in war zones and turns them into accurate, confronting reports — driving real changes in law and policy.

  • Human Rights Watch — since 1978
  • Rigorous, factual investigations
  • Direct recommendations to governments
06 — Nature

Of great value to health and economy

The natural world is crucial for both health and the economy. We support the World Wide Fund for Nature (WWF) — one of the world's largest conservation funds — working globally to fight climate change, make food sustainable, steward water, and protect forests, oceans and wildlife.

  • World Wide Fund for Nature (WWF)
  • Climate, water & sustainable food
  • Forests, oceans, flora & fauna
