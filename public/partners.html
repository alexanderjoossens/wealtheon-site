Working with partners

We work alongside independent advisers, family offices and wealth managers who want a dependable investment partner for their clients. The relationship is built on transparency, clear reporting and a shared long-term view — not on selling products. As an independent manager with no proprietary products beyond our own strategies, we manage your clients' assets the same way we manage our own.

One point of contact

Each partner works with a dedicated relationship manager for all investment, reporting and operational questions.

Full transparency

Complete visibility on holdings, transactions, performance and fees, so you can report to your own clients with confidence.

Flexible mandates

Discretionary mandates, fund investments or a combination — structured around your clients' legal, tax and operational needs.

Let's talk about working together

Tell us about your clients and how you work. We'll explain our approach and see whether a partnership makes sense.
