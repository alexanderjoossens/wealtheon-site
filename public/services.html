📈  Growth Strategy

The Growth strategy focuses on companies expected to grow their revenues and earnings significantly faster than the overall market average. These are typically younger, innovative businesses operating in expanding industries — technology, healthcare, consumer, and specialised industrials — where strong reinvestment of profits fuels compounding growth over time.

Growth investing demands patience and discipline. Short-term price volatility is an inherent feature of holding high-growth businesses. Our portfolio managers apply rigorous fundamental analysis to identify companies with durable competitive advantages, strong management, and a clear path to sustained profitability — not just temporary momentum.

Investment Philosophy

We look for companies that meet three key criteria:

  • Strong, proven revenue growth with visibility of continued expansion
  • Substantial reinvestment in research, technology, or market development
  • A clearly identifiable competitive moat protecting future returns
  • Management with skin in the game and a long-term orientation
  • Valuation discipline — even growth must be bought at a reasonable price

What to Expect

Clients invested in the Growth strategy should expect meaningful outperformance over full market cycles, with periods of underperformance during market drawdowns or risk-off environments. Our role is to manage that volatility through disciplined position sizing, diversification across sectors and geographies, and a clear-eyed assessment of valuation at all times.

Reporting is provided monthly, with a detailed quarterly letter from your portfolio manager reviewing each holding, the investment thesis, and any portfolio changes.

🛡️  Value Strategy

The Value strategy invests in solid, established companies with above-average return on equity that are temporarily available at prices below their intrinsic value. This approach is more defensive in nature — the discount to fair value provides a margin of safety that reduces downside risk and creates an asymmetric return profile over time.

Value investing is patient by design. Markets often misprice businesses due to short-term sentiment, temporary headwinds, or sector-wide pessimism. Our team uses deep fundamental research — not price momentum — to identify when a quality company is genuinely cheap.

Investment Philosophy

  • Quality businesses with proven profitability and strong balance sheets
  • Attractive valuations relative to earnings, book value, and free cash flow
  • A clear catalyst or path to value recognition over the medium term
  • Low financial leverage — capital preservation is always front of mind
  • Sector and geographic diversification to reduce concentration risk

What to Expect

Clients in the Value strategy typically experience smoother returns over time compared to growth-oriented mandates, with better protection during equity market downturns. The strategy aims to deliver consistent compounding — capturing a meaningful portion of market upside while limiting drawdowns.

Dividend income often plays a role in total return, as many value companies are mature businesses with strong cash generation and shareholder-friendly capital allocation.

🎯  High Conviction Strategy

The High Conviction strategy is Wealtheon's most concentrated offering — a focused portfolio of typically 15 to 25 positions drawn from across the growth and value universe. These are the investments where our conviction is highest: where the thesis is clear, the management is trusted, and the risk/reward is compelling.

Concentration is not recklessness — it is the result of saying "no" to everything except our very best ideas. Each position is sized to reflect our confidence in the thesis and the expected return profile. The portfolio is actively managed, with positions reviewed continuously against their original investment case.

Investment Philosophy

  • Only the highest-conviction ideas from across our research universe
  • Positions spanning both growth and value — wherever the best opportunities lie
  • No benchmark hugging — the portfolio looks genuinely different from market indices
  • Active position management with clear entry, sizing, and exit discipline
  • Full transparency: clients understand every position and its investment thesis

What to Expect

This strategy is best suited to investors who understand and embrace the nature of concentrated portfolios — higher potential for significant outperformance, with correspondingly higher short-term volatility relative to the market. Returns will deviate meaningfully from benchmark indices, both up and down.

Clients receive in-depth reporting including a position-by-position commentary, thesis updates, and a candid assessment of any mistakes or changes in view.

Partnerships

Financial Intermediaries

We work closely with independent financial advisers, family offices, wealth managers, and other financial intermediaries who seek a trusted investment management partner for their clients. Our intermediary model is built on genuine partnership, transparency, and aligned interests.

As an independent asset manager with no proprietary products beyond our own strategies, Wealtheon is free of the conflicts of interest that affect larger, bank-affiliated managers. We manage your clients' assets the same way we manage our own — with patience, rigour, and a long-term perspective.

Dedicated Support

Each intermediary partner is assigned a dedicated relationship manager at Wealtheon, providing a single point of contact for all operational, reporting, and investment matters.

Full Transparency

We provide complete transparency on portfolio composition, transaction history, performance attribution, and fee structures — enabling you to fulfil your own reporting obligations with ease.

White-Label Options

For qualifying intermediary partners, we can structure mandates and reporting under your branding, allowing you to deliver Wealtheon's investment quality seamlessly within your own client relationships.

Co-Investment

Consistent with our principle of aligned interests, Wealtheon staff co-invest in the strategies we manage — a meaningful demonstration of confidence in our own process.

Research Access

Intermediary partners have access to our in-house research, market views, and strategy updates — giving you deeper insight to share with your own clients.

Flexible Structures

We accommodate direct discretionary mandates, fund investments, and hybrid arrangements — whatever structure best suits your clients' legal, tax, and operational requirements.
